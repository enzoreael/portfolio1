My Story

Enzo - Young Creative Visionary

I'm a 15-year-old photographer and videographer with a passion for visual storytelling. My journey began when I got my first camera at 13, and since then, I've been completely hooked on capturing the world through my lens.

I specialize in automotive photography, landscapes, and cinematic video editing. There's something magical about being able to freeze a moment in time or tell a story through moving images.

When I'm not out shooting or editing, I'm learning new techniques, studying the work of photographers I admire, or experimenting with different styles and approaches to my craft.

This blog is where I share my journey, my favorite shots, and what I'm learning along the way. Thanks for stopping by!

My Photography Gear

Camera Body

I use a Sony A7III as my main camera. Its versatility and low-light performance make it perfect for all types of photography.

Lenses

My go-to lenses are the 24-70mm f/2.8 for versatility, and a 85mm f/1.8 for portraits and detail shots.

Video Equipment

For video, I use a DJI Ronin SC gimbal for smooth shots and a Rode VideoMic for better audio quality.

Editing Setup

I edit my photos in Lightroom and my videos in Premiere Pro. My color grading skills are constantly evolving!
